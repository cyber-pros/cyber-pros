Overview
CyberProsAI helps organizations govern AI agents before risk, regulation, and operational complexity outpace their ability to respond.

As organizations move from AI pilots to autonomous and semi-autonomous AI agents, governance can no longer stay theoretical. AI agents interact with data, systems, workflows, customers, employees, and decisions. That creates a new class of risk requiring practical oversight, clear accountability, and operational controls.

CyberProsAI specializes in Agentic AI Governance, AI GRC training, and advisory services that help organizations turn AI governance frameworks into real operating programs.

We help organizations answer critical questions:

Who is accountable when an AI agent takes action?
What controls are required before AI agents are deployed?
How should AI agent risks be assessed, documented, monitored, and escalated?
What evidence is needed to demonstrate responsible AI governance?
How do the NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 42001, and the EU AI Act become practical governance programs instead of policy documents?

Our advisory services help organizations assess AI risk posture, design governance frameworks, build oversight models, develop AI policies and controls, and prepare for regulatory readiness.

Our cohort-based training programs help professionals build practitioner-level AI GRC capability through real governance artifacts, including risk registers, control mappings, governance charters, AI policy drafts, agent oversight models, and compliance readiness roadmaps.

Core focus areas include:

Agentic AI Governance
AI GRC Training
AI Risk & Governance Assessments
AI Agent Oversight & Accountability
AI Compliance & Regulatory Readiness
NIST AI RMF Implementation
ISO/IEC 42001 Readiness
EU AI Act Readiness
AI Policy & Control Development
Governance Artifact Development

CyberProsAI exists to help organizations deploy AI agents with confidence, accountability, and control.
